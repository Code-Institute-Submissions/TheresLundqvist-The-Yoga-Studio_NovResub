About The Yoga Studio

We are one of Luleå's oldest and most experienced yoga studios with solid knowledge in the various branches of yoga.

Our goal is that you who come to us should feel safe, included and part of our community, while at the same time experiencing positive change in your life as a result of yoga.

We want to help you create the conditions for a yoga practice and a life that gives energy instead of taking and that creates a greater balance.



Welcome to the heat   

Our yoga classes are carefully designed so that everyone can take part in the classes and get the most out of their yoga practice.

All yoga is performed in pleasant warmth to give the body the opportunity to relax.

As a student with us, you should be able to safely participate in all classes and meet according to your needs.
